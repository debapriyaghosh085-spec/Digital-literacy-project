# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

- Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages
- Always verify website URLs before entering login details
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Never share OTPs or banking details with anyone
- Regularly update your passwords
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Be cautious while making UPI payments

# Financial Safety:
- Double-check UPI IDs before sending money
- Do not scan unknown QR codes

# Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
